Supermicro A2SDi-16C-HLN4F 16-core Intel Atom C3955 mITX

Wow, I somewhat like this board :-)



mobo:            Supermicro A2SDi-16C-HLN4F

cpu:             16 core Intel Atom C3955    (16M Cache, up to 2.40 GHz)
                 Single Socket FCBGA1310 supported
                 CPU TDP support 32W
                 16 cores, 16 threads / 16MB cache

ram:             Up to 256GB Registered ECC DDR4-2400MHz,
                 Up to 64GB Unbuffered ECC/Non-ECC DDR4-2400MHz; in 4 DIMM slots

Expansion slots: 1x PCI-E 3.0 x4                        <=== Only 1x slot

                 M.2 Interface: PCI-E 3.0 x2 and SATA 
                 M.2 Form Factor: 2242, 2280 
                 M.2 Key: M-Key

ssd:             Intel DC S3710 400GB
sata dom:        Supermicro 32GB SATADOM
OSes:            Windows Server 2016 Standard, Ubuntu Server 16.04.3 LTS, CentOS 7.3

ethernet:        4x 1GbE LAN ports

sata:            SoC controller for 12 SATA3 (6 Gbps) ports

i/o:             1 VGA, 1 COM, TPM header
                 1 USB 3.0 (Type-A) 
                 4 USB 2.0 (2 rear, 2 via headers)

display:         onboard vga

power:           24-pin ATX or DC input, i.e. standard PC power supply or an external power brick.

form factor:     6.7" x 6.7" (17.02cm x 17.02cm)
                 Mini-ITX

cooling:         Requires active cooling by chassis fan

ipmi:            IPMI2.0, KVM with dedicated LAN, NMI, SuperDoctor 5, Watchdog
